# /projectmaker — Complete Project Blueprint Generator

## What This Skill Does

This skill generates a **complete, production-ready project blueprint** as a PDF you can download. It adapts the proven IDERMA framework to YOUR product by asking 8-10 interactive questions, then outputting:

1. **Project Overview** — Executive summary of your product, audience, and strategy
2. **The 4 Customer Personas** — Segmentation + scoring dimensions specific to your product  
3. **Quiz Design** — 12-15 actual questions with scoring logic
4. **HTML Examples** — Ready-to-customize code for:
   - Landing page (with your product/pain points)
   - Quiz page (with your questions)
   - Results page (with your personas)
   - Checkout page (with your pricing)
5. **Project Architecture** — Folder structure, tech stack, automations
6. **Copy Templates** — Headlines, CTAs, and email flows for your product
7. **Launch Checklist** — 20-item pre-launch verification list
8. **Metrics & Timeline** — Expected conversions at each funnel stage

## How to Use It

1. **Start the skill** by saying something like:
   - "Help me design a project for [my product]"
   - "Build a funnel for my [product/service]"
   - "I have a [pain point] I want to solve with a business"

2. **Answer the interactive questions** — The skill will ask you about:
   - Your product/service
   - The main pain point it solves
   - Your target audience (age, gender, profession, location)
   - Pricing strategy
   - Brand tone and colors
   - Specific use cases or differentiators
   - Any existing competition or similar products

3. **Receive your PDF** — Download a complete 20-30 page blueprint with HTML code samples ready to customize

4. **Next steps** — Use the architecture section to:
   - Set up your tech stack (Google Sheets, Stripe, GHL, etc.)
   - Customize the HTML examples for your domain
   - Configure the quiz questions in your platform
   - Follow the pre-launch checklist before going live

## Important Notes

- **This is a BLUEPRINT, not a finished product.** All HTML/copy examples are templates you'll customize.
- **The skill uses the IDERMA framework as a base.** This means your product should have a systemic solution (not just cosmetic), affect daily life, and ideally have an emotional component. It works best for supplements, digital programs, coaching, health/wellness products, and B2C services.
- **You'll still need to:** Write your own copy, design graphics, set up payment processing, configure your CRM, and deploy. The blueprint just organizes the strategy.

## What Makes This Different

Unlike generic business plan templates, this skill:
- ✅ Generates **actual quiz questions** for your specific product
- ✅ Includes **HTML/CSS/JS code examples** you can copy-paste
- ✅ Provides **persona segmentation** not just audience description
- ✅ Shows **the full sales funnel flow** with automation points
- ✅ Includes **expected metrics** so you know what "good" looks like
- ✅ Adapts a **proven framework** (IDERMA, which was live and working) to your product
